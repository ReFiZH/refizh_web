ReFi Zuerich

The First ReFi Gathering in Zürich, Switzerland.

Be part of this journey!

Regenerating the planet is humanity's collective 21st-century art project.

ReFi Zürich is a community gathering, bringing together some of the most beautiful minds in art, technology, restoration, and social warriors—all connected by the spirit of Web3 and ReFi. We want to foster an environment for the emerging ReFi community to meet and grow. We embrace diversity and the creative chaos that comes from such an event.
The magic always happens on the fringes.
